Announcing Our Modern Laboratory & Superior Patient Care Facilities!

Laminar Operation Theatre

A Laminar Flow Operation Theatre is a highly specialized surgical room designed to maintain a sterile environment. It uses advanced air filtration systems to create a continuous flow of clean, particle-free air over the surgical site. This helps to minimize the risk of infection and ensures optimal conditions for delicate surgical procedures.

DR X-Ray

DR X-ray is a revolutionary imaging technique that significantly enhances diagnostic accuracy. By providing high-resolution images, faster acquisition times, and reduced radiation exposure, DR X-ray empowers healthcare professionals to make informed decisions and deliver optimal patient care.

C-Arm

A C-Arm is a type of X-ray machine that is used to obtain real-time images during surgical procedures. It consists of an X-ray source and a detector mounted on a C-shaped arm that can be rotated around the patient. This allows for dynamic imaging, enabling surgeons to visualize the surgical site from multiple angles. C-Arms are commonly used in orthopedic, vascular, and interventional radiology procedures.

Ultra-Sound Scan

Ultrasound scans are a safe and painless imaging technique that uses sound waves to create images of your internal organs. No radiation is involved, making it safe for people of all ages, including pregnant women. The procedure is quick and often requires no special preparation. Your healthcare provider will explain the specific details of your scan and any necessary instructions.

Laproscopy

Laparoscopic surgery is a minimally invasive surgical technique that involves making small incisions to perform surgical procedures. This method often leads to faster recovery times, less pain, and smaller scars compared to traditional open surgery.

AC Room Facility

A well-appointed hospital room with air conditioning provides a comfortable and conducive environment for patients to recover. The controlled temperature and air quality can help reduce discomfort, promote healing, and enhance overall patient satisfaction.
